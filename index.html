Our Story

Our Beginning

Digital Aksumite was founded on the visionary ideas of Biniam Shimeles, who sought to revolutionize Ethiopia's technological landscape. This vision was later shared with co-founder Solomon Eshetu, and in 2017 EC (2024 GC), the PLC was officially established.

Our Vision

Inspired by the Aksumite civilization, we envision empowering Ethiopia through the creation of systems that define, protect, and guide society. Our ultimate goal is to systematize and modernize Ethiopia, making it a global model of excellence.
